About iTalkBB

iTalkBB is a company dedicated to creating innovative and authentic mobile communication experiences and TV content for people around the globe. We provide international phone services that break down distance barriers and allows our customers to easily and affordably connect with their communities – no matter where they may be. Our headquarters is located in Washington D.C., but we maintain branch offices all over the world, in locations such as Canada, Australia, Singapore, China, and more.

Our Mission

"Life is good everywhere" is our mission statement. In order to achieve this mission, iTalkBB focuses on delivering convenient, cost-effective, and unlimited talk, text and data services to foreign-born individuals living in the United States, Canada, Australia, and Singapore, who regularly call or text internationally to stay connected with family and friends around the globe.

We are dedicated to providing more products and services tailored to our meet our customers' needs and to build a better life for them, no matter where they may be. We understand our customer base and work hard to tailor our products to fulfill their individual needs. We strive to develop meaningful and mutually rewarding partnerships with our customers, business partners and co-workers by creating differentiated communication and television products that better our communities, our families, and our business partners as a whole.

Our Size

Currently, iTalkBB has become the No.1 telecom company in both the overseas Chinese market and the overseas Korean market all over the globe. Over 30 iTalkBB retail stores have spread across the U.S., Canada, Australia, Singapore, etc., with more than 1.2 million iTalkBB users located around the world.

In the U.S., there is one iTalkBB user in every five Chinese people and our brand awareness is over 80% in Chinese communities.

Our Services

iTalkBB Mobile – Best Value Mobile Service.

iTalkBB offers the best value mobile plans that allows users to enjoy a world of communication with unlimited talk, text, and data, including 4G LTE high-speed data in both the U.S. and China.

iTalkBB Chinese TV – Best Chinese TV Service.

iTalkBB Chinese TV provides the highest quality and most popular Chinese TV content with copyright granted. It includes over 60 live channels and 72-hour playback, the most popular Chinese movies, TV series and variety shows in HD quality on demand. Moreover, iTalkBB Chinese TV is dedicated to collecting the best TV content that overseas Chinese are interested in, enabling overseas users to enjoy high quality Chinese entertainment no matter where they are.

iTalkBB Home Phone – 14 Years Experience in International Phone Services.

In addition to high-quality national and international phone calls, we provide a unique function based on the requirements of our foreign-born customers, who regularly call or text internationally to stay connected with family and friends around the globe. For individuals in the U.S. who have famlies and friends in Mainland China, Taiwan, Hong Kong and Korea, they are able to call their families and friends directly with their Chinese, Taiwan, Hong Kong or Korean phone numbers without paying toll charges if their families and friends are also iTalkBB users. The iTalkBB home phone service beats traditional home phones by providing many additional functions for free—e.g. call waiting, three-way calling, caller ID, call forwarding, etc.

Our Logo

In July 2016, in addition to our original English brand name, iTalkBB released its new Chinese brand name "蜻蜓"（Qingting） and logo. In Chinese, the pronunciation of "Qingting" is "listen carefully", which reflects iTalkBB's respect for each of our users, our dedication in listening to every user, and our mission to provide the kinds of products and services that meet the needs of overseas Chinese.

Our logo is made up of two dragonflies in flight, which represents the culture of overseas immigrants and that of the countries they relocate to fitting together in harmony. The two dragonflies form an interlocking circle, symbolizing iTalkBB's hope to connect the global world.

The new brand name and logo demonstrates iTalkBB's dedication to better serving its foreign-born customers. With the new Chinese brand name and logo, iTalkBB wants to spread its message to broader markets and new customers: life is good everywhere.

iTalkBB Milestones

2003 - iTalkBB home phone service in Texas, U.S.A. was launched

2005 - U.S. nationwide home phone service was launched

2006 - Canada nationwide home phone service was launched

2008 - iTalkBB entered the Korean market in North America

2008 - iTalkBB and the Yao Ming Foundation formed a partnership

2009 - iTalkBB entered the Australian and Singaporean markets

2012 - Olympic fencing champion Sheng Lei became the brand ambassador of iTalkBB

2012 - iTalkBB Chinese TV service launched

2016 - April, iTalkBB and ICNTV cooperated strategically to build the copyright-granted Chinese TV platform

2016 - May, iTalkBB and Hisense cooperated strategically to grant access to iTalkBB Chinese TV contents on Hisense smart TVs

2016 - July, iTalkBB's new Chinese brand name "蜻蜓" (Qingting) and logo were revealed

2016 - October, iTalkBB became the official strategic partner of the Brooklyn Nets

2017 - January, iTalk Global Communication, Inc. acquired iTalkBB Media to accelerate the TV advertising platform

2017 - March, iTalkBB mobile business launched in the U.S.

2017 - June, the movie contents of iTalkBB Chinese TV were fully upgraded, and iTalkBB was granted exclusive copyrights for Reset, The Adventurers and Girls 2 in North America
