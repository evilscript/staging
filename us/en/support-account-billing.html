Account suspended due to unpaid bills (iTalkBB Mobile only): If iTalkBB fails to charge your credit card on your billing day, your phone service will be suspended immediately. If you do not pay your bill for more than 45 days, your account will be revoked.

About Refer a Friend

With our Refer a Friend Program, you will be rewarded for bringing a friend or family member over to iTalkBB. The individual you refer also earns a reward - it's a win-win situation! Learn more about Refer a Friend.

How to Refer

To refer a friend, clickhere and enter your friend’s email address. iTalkBB will send them a sign-up email. If the person you refer signs up for iTalkBB's services through the email link and retains the service for over 30 days, both of you are eligible for rewards. Alternatively, if your friend purchases the service online, in-store, or over the phone, he/she only needs to enter or give your 899 number to the representative in order to earn rewards for the both of you.

How Referral Credits Work

Referral credits can be used to pay for your monthly service plan and any add-on features you choose. Login to your account, click “Rewards” in the menu bar at the top of the page, and navigate to the “Reward Management” page to redeem your credits.

How to Receive a Reward

The number of people you refer will be calculated each calendar month. Once the number of referrals meets the amount required by the program and they retain the service for over 30 days, we will begin processing your reward gift. It will take 15 business days to deliver from the date your reward request begins to process.

Check Your Bill

iTalkBB allows you to easily manage your billing statements by logging into your account online. After logging in, click "Billing Information" in the menu at the top of the page to view your latest bill.

If you want to check previous billing statements, click "Billing Information" in the menu at the top of the page, then go into "Billing History" to access your bills for the last 6 months.

Read Your Bill

Billing period:After account activation, the following 30 days will be regarded as one billing period.

Payment:You will not need to make the payment manually after receiving your bill.

We will automatically collect your plan's monthly fee for the next month and the additional service charges incurred for the prior month on your payment date.

Using Both iTalkBB Mobile Services and iTalkBB Home Phone Services

After activating iTalkBB Mobile services, we will charge your mobile service from the date of activation to the end of your regular home phone billing period. The next month's period will be the same as your regular home phone billing period.

Payment Methods

We only accept debit and credit card payments, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express and Discover.

Payment Date

The service cycle is every 30 days, with the date on which your account was activated as the starting date of your billing period. The 30 days following the starting date are regarded as one billing period. We will automatically collect your plan's monthly fee for the next month and the additional service charges incurred for the prior month on your payment date.

Please note: For iTalkBB Home Phone users who have applied for iTalkBB Mobile services later, we will collect your first mobile service fees based on your service usage from the date of activation to the end of your regular home phone billing period. Then, the next month's billing period of your mobile service will automatically start at the same date as your regular home phone billing period.

Payment Failed

If iTalkBB fails to charge your debit or credit card, your service will be suspended. If you do not make payment on the late bill for over 45 days, your account and phone number will be revoked (iTalkBB Mobile only). Any remaining balance in the account is non-refundable and cannot be transferred to another account. To avoid suspension or revocation of your account, please keep your credit card information up to date. If there is already an unpaid bill on your account, please make payment on the bill after updating your credit card information. Pay your bills now? You can choose to log into your account online, or contact our 24/7 support service to make a payment.

Please note: Please note: For first time users, login information is included in your order confirmation email. If you have not received this email, please contact us.
