iTalkBB Home Phone users can get an 11-digit “virtual China local number” beginning with 950. Friends in the U.S. can contact you using your U.S. number, while friends and family in China can contact you via your 950 number directly, avoiding international call tolls. Users can also make international calls without being charged a toll. This is an exclusive feature offered by iTalkBB that saves countless toll charges for iTalkBB users as well as their friends in China.

Cheap Calls from Taiwan Without Toll Charges

iTalkBB Home Phone provides each user with a Taiwan phone number for free. Your friends and family in Taiwan can call this phone number, then call your 899 number to get in touch with you at any time. Families and friends in Taiwan do not have to pay any tolls when calling you using this method.

Caller ID

Caller ID

Caller ID displays the name and number of the current caller on your telephone screen.

Call Forwarding

Call Forwarding

You can choose to forward your calls to a different telephone number.

Call Waiting

Call Waiting

If a second call comes in while you are already on a call, you will hear a tone, at which point you may choose to suspend the call already in progress to accept the second call, or you may choose to remain on the first call.

Three-Way Calling

Three-Way Calling

Whether you make or receive the initial call, you can use Three-Way Calling to add a third person to your call at any time.

How to Connect Your Three-Way Call:

1. Please dial the number of the first caller the same as when you're making a regular call

2. Press the Answer key or the Flash key

3. When you hear the dial tone, input the telephone number of the third party in the conversation

4. After connecting to the third party, press the Answer key or the Flash key again to connect the three-way call

Voice Mail

Voice Mail

Voicemail is a service that lets callers leave a voice message if you aren't available to answer your phone. iTalkBB Home Phone also provides you with the option to receive your voicemails via email.

Fax

Fax

iTalkBB Home Phone provides you with a free domestic fax service, allowing you to send and receive faxes at your convenience.
