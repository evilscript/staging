[US]iTalkBB Announces Company Expansion

05/09/2006

iTalk Global Communications, Inc. (iTalkBB), the pioneer in the Voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP) industry announces results from their 10-month campaign to outreach to the global Chinese market in the United States and Canada. Sales have exceeded 1000% growth. iTalkBB attributes the success to offering unique services tailored for the Chinese market with competitive pricing structures. Customers replace their traditional phone service plans, without changing their phone equipment, or changing their current phone numbers

The company is working on expansion plans into the Asian markets, in China and Taiwan. In the next quarter, iTalkBB will be providing services for other Asian speaking communities in North America.

It is only natural that iTalkBB, a true pioneer in the industry, grew to be the best VoIP company and on the leading edge."Our network carried the first VoIP call to China," says Jack Zhao, President and CEO.

Commitment, honesty, and hard work are the core values of the executive management team at iTalkBB. Understanding the needs of the customers is the key to iTalkBB's success."We study and listen to our customers, and we work to design services that make their lives easier," says Zhao."Serving the global Chinese community and giving them the freedom to communicate, without the boundaries of countries and continents, continues to be our goal. iTalkBB has a unique feature designed only for our customers, a free China telephone number. This allows friends and family in China to call you in the United States and Canada by dialing the China phone number assigned to you, without paying international long distance charges," explains Zhao.

There is a menu of calling plans tailored for residential or business usage. Advanced features such as call waiting, three-way calling, caller ID, call forwarding and more are included in the plans at no extra charge."iTalkBB is the largest VoIP provider to tens of thousands of Chinese users in North America," says Sizhen Wang, Executive Vice President, Marketing and Sales."Our consumer division has telephone plans that allow you to replace your current service from traditional phone companies. Our customers enjoy upgraded calling features and receive savings of about 50% to 90% off their monthly telephone bills," says Wang.

iTalkBB is Sina.Com Online's only North American VoIP partner. The joint marketing effort is based on the two companies' mutual pledge of excellence in service and product for Chinese clients. iTalkBB and Sina.Com Online offer co-branded advertising, and information can be found at their joint website www.italkbb.sina.com.

iTalkBB is a subsidiary of iTalk Global Communications, Inc., a provider of high quality broadband phone services at competitive prices and a leader in the international telecommunications industry. The executive offices are in Vienna, Virginia. The technical and engineering offices are in Austin, Texas, and 24-hour call centers are in Beijing, and Shanghai, China.
