iTalkBB Announces Affinity Partnership with Sina.com Online

(Vienna, VA, September 27)

09/27/2005

Firms Offer Services Tailored for the Global Chinese Market.

iTalk Global Communications, Inc. (iTalkBB), the pioneer in the Voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP) industry announce their affinity partnership with Sina.Com Online, a subsidiary of SINA Corporation, operates in the United States with a Chinese-language web site at http://home.sina.com that provides, among other services, locally-tailored news, entertainment, and lifestyle content.

As the community here continues to grow, so do demands for services that are tailored for the Chinese consumers. iTalkBB with Sina.Com Online have joined together to deliver highly focused services that meet the needs of the Chinese consumer. These companies offer websites in Chinese, customer service support staff speaking Chinese, and services with features for the Chinese population in the United States.

iTalkBB is Sina.Com Online's only North American VoIP partner. The joint marketing effort is based on two companies' mutual pledge to excellence in service and product. iTalkBB is a subsidiary of iTalk Global Communications, Inc., a provider of high quality broadband phone services at competitive prices and a leader in the international telecommunications industry. Visit the iTalkBB website at www.italkbb.com.

“We truly are pioneers in our industry. Our network carried the first VoIP call to China,” said CEO Jack Zhao. “We want to offer smart choices for our customers. iTalkBB's consumer division has telephone plans that allow you to replace your current service from traditional phone companies. Our customers enjoy upgraded calling features and save money, too. Our customers see savings of about 50% to 90% off their monthly telephone bills.”

“Like Sina.Com Online, we understand our Chinese clients. iTalkBB has a unique feature designed only for our customers. We give you a free China telephone number. This allows friends and family in China to call you in the United States by dialing a China phone number assigned to you, without paying international long distance charges,” adds Zhao.

iTalkBB and Sina.Com Online will offer co-branded advertising and information can be found at their joint website www.italkbb.sina.com.Currently, these two firms are partnering with American online leading brokerage firm Scottrade, Inc. Asia Pacific to offer a new account gift promotion program. Once you open an account with Scottrade, or refer your friends and family to open accounts at Scottrade, you will receive iTalkBB free broadband phone services, including a free China phone number and 1,000 minutes free long distance talk time.The package of the gift is worth over 160 US dollars. This offer will expire at the end of November. Details can be found on the websitehttp://italkbb.sina.com/scottrade/.
