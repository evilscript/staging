iTalkBB Offers Competitive Pricing to Ethnic Markets

(Vienna, VA, January 1)

01/01/2005

Focuses on Global Chinese Market with Breakthrough Features

iTalk Global Communications, Inc. (iTalkBB) announce the company's marketing focus will be to serve the ethnic communities, with initial marketing efforts on the global Chinese population. As the multicultural communities grow around the world, the need for these communities to place calls to family, friends, and business associates in their native countries increases.

iTalkBB is positioned to provide high quality Voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP) services at competitive prices. iTalkBB is a subsidiary of iTalk Global Communications, Inc., a pioneer in the VoIP revolution and a leader in the international telecommunications industry.

"In terms of rates and customer service, we have found current telecommunications companies under serve the Chinese population in the United States and Canada," said Sizhen Wang, Executive Vice President of Marketing and Sales. "iTalkBB is powered by our company's state of the art technologies and international network. Our customers enjoy clear, high quality local, long distance, and international calling at a fraction of what they pay today." Mr. Wang adds, "Plans are underway to tailor services focused on the Chinese communities. We will have very exciting calling features to unveil in the next few months."

iTalkBB's residential and business service plans offer unlimited, unrestricted calling in North America for the lowest monthly rates available. International calling rates are consistently lower than other telecommunication companies and international phone cards. Rates to China are only 2 cents per minute.
