iTalkBB Opens Second Customer Service Center in China Customer Growth

(Vienna, VA, June 8)

06/08/2005

Customer Growth Demands Live Support Staff Fluent in Chinese

iTalk Global Communications, Inc.'s (iTalkBB) focus to serve the global Chinese population has placed a heavy demand on the customer service center in Tysons Corner, Virginia.

"We are pleased to announce that our Chinese American population is enthralled with our new and unique service features. Our customer base is growing so rapidly, that in order to properly serve our clients, we opened a Live Support Staff Customer Service Center in Beijing, China," said Jack Zhou, CEO of iTalkBB.

iTalkBB provides high quality Voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP) services at prices competitive to traditional phone company services. iTalkBB is a subsidiary of iTalk Global Communications, Inc., a pioneer in the VoIP revolution and a leader in the international telecommunications industry.

"One primary differentiator that separates us from our competitors is that our focus is to serve the global multicultural communities," said Sizhen Wang, Executive Vice President of Marketing and Sales. "The Chinese American communities prefer Chinese as their primary means of communications. We offer a bilingual website, live support staff fluent in several Chinese dialects, and all our collateral materials in Chinese and English."

iTalkBB also wants to involve the community in the growth of the company. "In addition to our great calling plans, we also offer Referral and Affiliate programs that allow our customers to earn credits on their service bills," Wang explains. "We will also help people interested in launching profitable businesses to sell our services. For full details, email iTalkBB at partnership@iTalkBB.com"

For additional information, visit www.iTalkBB.com
