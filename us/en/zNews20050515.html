iTalkBB Offers Customers a Unique China Phone Number

(Vienna, VA, May 15)

05/15/2005

Breakthrough VoIP Feature for the Global Chinese Market

2005 iTalk Global Communications, Inc. (iTalkBB) is finding success in their initial marketing of Voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP) services to the Chinese population in the United States. This population is one of the top five ethnic categories placing long distance and international calls in America.

iTalkBB is positioned to provide high quality VoIP services at competitive prices. iTalkBB is a subsidiary of iTalk Global Communications, Inc., a pioneer in the VoIP revolution and a leader in the international telecommunications industry.

"We are focusing on developing services that meet the needs of the global Chinese community," said Sizhen Wang, Executive Vice President of Marketing and Sales. "Our newest feature is our unique 'iTalkBB China Phone Number' and it speaks directly to the needs of our Chinese customers. We want to extend our products and services to encompass the entire "family of users', from the United States to overseas." Wang explains, "It is very expensive for callers in China to make international calls to the US or other countries. iTalkBB gives you a free China telephone number when you establish your account. You give this number to your friends, family, and business associates in China. This allows the caller, in all major cities in China, to simply dial your local China number to reach you. You receive the call, in the United States, and the caller in China has not incurred any domestic or international long distance rates."

The 'iTalkBB China Phone Number' is similar to owning a toll free number in the United States. Calls are billed at a low rate of only two cents per minute.
