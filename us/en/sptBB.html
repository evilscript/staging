How to install iTalkBB Home Phone with router:

  • Plug one end of the cable into the "Internet" jack of the iTalkBB Home Phone Box and the other end into the Ethernet (LAN) socket on your home router
  • Plug one end of you telephone line into the "Phone 1" jack of the iTalkBB Home Phone Box and the other end into your telephone
  • Connect iTalkBB Home Phone Box to power

How to install iTalkBB Home Phone with modem (without router):

  • Disconnect the original modem from the PC, plug one of the network cables into the "Internet" jack of the iTalkBB Home Phone Box, and the other at the Modem's "LAN" socket
  • Connect one end of the cable to the "LAN" jack of the iTalkBB Home Phone Box and the other end to the computer's "Ethernet" jack
  • Plug one end of the telephone lines into the "Phone 1" jack of the iTalkBB Home Phone Box and the other end into your telephone
  • Connect the iTalkBB Home Phone Box to power

How to dial:

Making International Calls with home phone:

  • Call a US or Canada number: 1 + area number + telephone number, Example: 1-202-888-8888
  • Call an international number: Please dial 011 + country code + phone number, Example: 011-86-10-88888888
  • Call to other iTalkBB home phone users using the free in-network call service: dial the 899 number
  • Fax to United States, Canada: Please call # + 1 + fax number, Example: # + 1 + 888-888-8888

How to Transfer Your Home Phone Number

There are two ways to transfer your home phone number:

  • - Apply for phone number transferring service online by completing Letter of Authorization
    http://www.italkbb.com/bb/chs/Transfer/chs_Number_Transfer.asp；
  • Or send your current home phone bill to Portin@iTalkBB.com or fax it to 1-800-872-2059

While your home phone number is in the process of porting to iTalkBB, please note:

  • Your name, address and phone number have to be on the first page of your bill and have to be consistent with Letter of Authorization
  • The name and address you submit for phone number transfer must be the same with the ones you provided to your currentl telephone company, i.e. Service Address. If you are not sure, please contact your current company to confirm the CSR (Customer service record) information for your phone number. If the information you provide is inaccurate, your phone number transferring application will be rejected.
  • Please do not cancel your current home phone service during the transfer process, otherwise, the number will be revoked.
  • After successfully transferring your phone number, your current home phone services will be canceled automatically. In case, you can double check with your current telephone company beforehand.

Check your transfer/port request status

The process will take about 2-6 weeks. We will inform you via email of the latest updates. You can also log in to your online account or call 1-877-482-5503 to check the status.

Successful Transfer

Once your home phone number transfers to iTalkBB successfully, your previous home phone service will be automatically canceled.

Failed Transfer

Most of the home phone numbers can be successfully transferred. However, but for users who have bundle plans of home phone and Internet services, they can not cancel home phone service with Internet service retained. Therefore, these users have to confirm with current home phone service provider before transferring home phone number.

Call Transfer - How to set up:

> Set up to transfer all calls: After you hear the dial tone, press "*" +72 to enter the phone number you need to set up. After you hear a short tone, the setup is complete.

> Cancel to transfer all calls: After you hear the dial tone, press the "*" key +73. After you hear a short tone, the cancellation is complete.

> Set up to transfer all unanswered calls: After you hear the dial tone, press the "*" key +92 and the phone number you need to set up. Then when you hear a short tone, the process is complete.

> Cancel to transfer all unanswered calls: After you hear the dial tone, press the "*" key +93. After you hear a tone, the cancellation is complete.

Telephone call troubleshooting

The phone has no dial tone

  • Please make sure that your network works;
  • Make sure your phone box is installed properly

    The normal status is:

    > power light, network light, Phone 1 light are on

    > Phone 1 light flashing during the call, the network light sometimes flashes

    > If the telephone box is not installed properly, please click here for troubleshooting

The phone can call out but cannot receive calls

  • Please confirm that your home phone is activated within 24 hours or you transfer your home phone number within 24 hours; iTalkBB home phone usually works immediately after activation or phone number transferring. But in some cases, it may need 24 hours before it works.
  • Make sure that your telephone line is connected to the "Phone 1" socket of the iTalkBB telephone box rather than "Phone 2".
  • If you set up some advanced features for your home phone, it may also lead to some problems with the phone. Please make sure that the dial tone is normal: if you hear some abnormal tones like 3 short tones plus 1 long tone, please try to cancel the following special features.

    Instructions for operation:

    > Cancel Call Forwarding - Pick up the phone, press * 73

    > Cancel Call Rejection - Pick up the phone, press * 79

    > Cancel direct accessing to voicemail - Pick up the phone, press * 993

The phone can receive calls but cannot call out

  • Please make sure the phone number you input is correct, for example, you need to dial 011 + country code + phone number of your relatives or friends when you make an international call. Click here for more information.
  • Please make sure if there is nothing wrong with your phone box. You can try to replace the phone box, or restart the telephone.

Poor Calling Quality

  • Please confirm that your network is normal. Usually, if you can hear others speak clearly, but they can not hear you speak clearly, the poor calling quality may be caused by your network. Downloading software or other activities use network bandwidth and will cause the problem.
  • If you hear "rust" or static noise, it may be due to poor contact of your telephone receiver or telephone line, please replace the telephone line and reconnect it.
  • If your phone is connected to extensions without disconnecting the Local bus, there will be noise, echo, etc.; Please disconnect the Local bus before connecting extensions.
  • If you are using a cordless phone, please change to another frequency to see if the call quality is improved.
  • If all of the above fails, try restarting the iTalkBB telephone box.

Problems Related to Telephone Box Installation

All the indicator lights are off

  • ITalkBB phone box is installed correctly when:

    > Power light, network light, Phone 1 light are on

    > Phone 1 light flashing during the call, the network light sometimes flashes

  • 3 lights are all off showing that the box does not work. Please check power connection, and try to replace the power outlet. If the power supply is available and the power supply is connected but the problem still happens, there should be something wrong with the equipment. Please contact us for help.

Only the power light is on

  • ITalkBB phone box is installed correctly when: power light, network light, Phone 1 light are on; Phone 1 light flashing during the call, the network light sometimes flashes.
  • If only the power light is on, it is a connection problem, please follow the steps:

    > Please check if the cable is incorrectly inserted into the yellow ethernet interface of iTalkBB phone box. It should be connected to the blue Internet interface.

    > Please make sure that your home modem/router/hub is working properly, please try different ports.

    > Please make sure the network cable works, try different network cable.

The power light and the network light are on and the Phone 1 light flashes

The reason for that including the phone does not hang up or there are some problems with extensions, please follow the steps:

> Please check if your telephone has hung up.

> Unplug the telephone line from the phone box to see if the Phone 1 light is still on for a long time. If yes, it is the problem of phone line or extensions; if the Phone 1 light flashes, there may be some problems with the phone box.

Power light, network light, Phone 1 light are on, but the phone does not work

Please check if the phone box works, try to replace the phone box or telephone line. If the Phone 1 light is still on but no flashes, there may be something wrong with the phone box.

Problems Related to Functions

Caller IDs do not display

  • If some of caller IDs display but some do not, it may because some callers have set up blocking function
  • If all the caller IDs do not display, make sure your phone battery and display screen work, or you can try to change the phone box
  • Please make sure that your caller ID function is on: pick up the phone, press * 65 to turn on the caller ID function; press * 85 to turn off the caller ID function

Three-party call troubleshooting

  • Make sure the way you use the three-way call is correct. Instructions for three-party call: connect to someone first, then press the answer key or the Flash key. After you hear a dialing tone, input the phone number of the third party in this conversation. When you connect to the third person, press the answer key or the Flash key again to start the three-party call.
  • iTalkBB home phone only supports three-party call among Mainland China (excluding Hong Kong, Macao, and Taiwan), the United States, Canada, Britain, Singapore, Australia
  • If all the above solutions do not work, try to restart iTalkBB phone box

Fax does not work normally

  • Please confirm that you use the function in the correct way: press # 1 + the fax number of receiver; if it still does not work normally, try to use 333 + 1 + the fax number of receiver, * 99 + 1 + the fax number of receiver
  • If you are using a Cable network, connect the phone box directly to the modem or Router and try again
  • Please try to change the fax speed to 9600 bps or less
  • Please turn off Error Correction Mode of your fax machine
