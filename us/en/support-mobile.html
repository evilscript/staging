Product Features

Each iTalkBB mobile phone plan has the following features:

  • Unlimited text
  • Three-way call
  • Call waiting
  • Unlimited international text
  • Voicemail - Set up your voicemail

  • iTalkBB Mobile App

Download iTalkBB App to use voicemail, or press and hold 1 in the dialing interface to connect to voicemail, and then set up voicemail following the voice instruction.

All iTalkBB mobile plans offer unlimited data. However, unlimited data and high-speed data are different. Each mobile plan provides a limited amount of high-speed 4G LTE data per month. When your 4G LTE high-speed data is used up, 3G data automatically kicks in, but the speed will decline. Even with reduced speed, you will still be able to send emails, browse online, and chat online normally.

If you choose a 5GB mobile plan, that means you will have 5GB of high speed data to use. After the high speed data is used up, you will still be able to utilize the network services albeit at a lower speed within the same billing period.

About Your iTalkBB Mobile Plan

"China and U.S. SIM Cards” Plan

Users with the “China and U.S. SIM cards” plan have unlimited local call and text in China and the U.S.

> SIM card fee: $10/U.S. SIM card, $ 10/China SIM card; China card service fee: $12/year.

> Users can enjoy unlimited calls, text and data in China or U.S. Data will be shared in both countries. For example: If you have 5GB of high-speed data per month, you will use up to 5GB in high speed data in the U.S. and China.

> Your U.S. and China phone SIM card are one-to- one binding. When you are in the United States, the call to your China number will automatically be transferred to your U.S. number; when you are in China, the call to your U.S. number will automatically be transferred to your China number. No matter which SIM card you use, you will not miss any incoming calls to another card, and the caller only needs to pay as local calls.

> Ways to Bind 2 Cards:
Online account: Login to youronline account, bind your phone numbers and choose to add your China SIM card.
Contact us: Call us at 1-855-482-5566, and our professional customer service staff will help you bind your two phone numbers together.
Retail store: Visit anyiTalkBB retail store, and the staff will be happy to help you bind your two phone numbers together.

Transfer Your Number to iTalkBB

New and existing users can easily transfer existing numbers or add new numbers to iTalkbb mobile network.

How to transfer your number:

  • You will need to provide the following information:

    > Your Mobile Number

    > The account ID of your current mobile operator

    > The password of your current mobile operator account

    > The address and zip code of your current mobile operator account

  • Select the following ways to keep your original number:

    > Call us at 1-855-482-5566

    > Visit any iTalkBB retail store

  • After submitting the application, iTalkBB will send you a SIM card. After receiving the new phone card, please call us at1-855-482-5566 to link your iTalkBB mobile plan with your original phone number. While your mobile phone number is in the process of porting to iTalkBB, please note:

    > Your original SIM card may still receive incoming calls and texts even after you linked your number to the new SIM card.

    > Your web service will not be affected. Once you activate your new SIM card, you can start to use data services right away.

    > In most cases, the phone number transfer process will be completed within 24 hours. If you want to check your number transfer/port request status, please call us at 1-855-482-5566.

    Note: The mobile number transfer process utilizes the information provided by the user. iTalkBB contacts the user’s current mobile operator to keep the original phone number. Therefore, the information the user provides to iTalkBB must be exactly the same as the information on the user’s account with their current mobile operator. If the information does not match, we will not be able to complete the transfer process. Please do not cancel your current mobile plan before the transfer process is completed as your phone number will be revoked by your current mobile operator if you do so and we will not be able to transfer it. Moreover, we recommend that you keep a copy of the bill from your current mobile operator. The password for your current mobile operator may be different from the password for your online account or voicemail. If you cannot remember the password for your current mobile operator, you can contact your current mobile operator with the information on your bill.

Network Connection Failure

When you use mobile services within areas that are covered by 3G, 4G, or 4G LTE networks, your mobile phone will automatically search for the best network connection. When the phone has successfully connected to the 3G, 4G or 4G LTE network, 3G, 4G, H or 4G LTE will appear at the upper left corner or upper right corner of your phone.

If your mobile phone fails to connect to a network, please check the following:

> Your mobile phone supports 3G, 4G, or 4G LTE networks

> Your phone has been updated to the latest software version

> There is a mobile network in your area

> You've restarted your phone

> Your SIM card is inserted properly into the device

> You've tried manually searching for a network

> Other iTalkBB 3G, 4G, or 4G LTE mobile devices are working properly in your area

If you are still unable to connect to a mobile network, please visit aniTalkBB retail store or call us at 1-855-482-5566. Our professional customer service staff will be able to assist you with any questions.

Unable to Make or Receive Calls

Please check the following:

> If your mobile phone has no signal, please refer to Network Connection Failure

> You have paid all outstanding bills

> Turn off your cell phone for 5 seconds and then restart it

> Remove SIM card and reload it again

If the problem remains unresolved, please visit any of our iTalkBB retail store locations or call us at 1-855-482-5566. Our professional customer service staff will be able to assist you with any questions.

Unable to Dial a Specific Number

Please check the following:

> If you are using a third-party application or dialing software (such as Google Voice, etc.), please exit the application and try your call again.

> If you are dialing an international number, please make sure you have enabled the international calling features and you are calling with the correct country code.

> Try turning off your cell phone for 5 seconds and then restarting it.

> If the problem occurs only when dialing a specific number, please redial this number in the call dialing interface rather than from the recent calls interface.

> Try using a different phone to dial the number to see if the call can be connected.

> If all of the above methods fail, please try to contact the operator of that number as there may be a problem with their telephone number.

If the problem remains unresolved, please visit any of our iTalkBB retail store locations or call us at 1-855-482-5566. Our professional customer service staff will be able to assist you with any questions.
