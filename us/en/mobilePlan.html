iTalkBB Mobile offers you both a local U.S. and a local China SIM card on one plan. The U.S. SIM card is for you to use while in the United States, and the China SIM card is for you to use while in China. The two SIM cards share one mobile phone plan, so you can enjoy unlimited local calls, text and data whether you are in the U.S. or in China.

When Using the U.S. SIM Card in the U.S.

You can enjoy unlimited local calls, text and high-speed data3.

When friends and relatives in China call your Chinese mobile phone number, the calls will automatically be transferred to your U.S. mobile phone, and your families and friends in China only pay local call fees.

When you use the U.S. SIM card in the U.S., you can also receive text messages and verification codes from China! Even if you are in the U.S., you can conveniently and easily handle any business from China!

When Using the China SIM Card in China

You can enjoy unlimited local calls, text and high-speed data.

When you use the China SIM card in China, you can also receive calls from your U.S. SIM card. Friends and families in the U.S. calling your U.S. mobile phone number will automatically be transferred to your Chinese mobile phone number at no additional cost.
