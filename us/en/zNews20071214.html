Calls from China to your China Phone Number through your iTalkFreedom service will have a rate increase from $0.02/min to $0.04/min. (This change does not apply to calls to your BB phones. Your friends and family in China Homeland callback to you still without paying long distance charge). We apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused, and hope this will not affect your use of our iTalkFreedom service.

Please note:

1. iTalkFreedom is an add on service to your iTalkBB residential service plans, it is not bonded with your residential plan service contract. You may cancel it at anytime simply by logging into your account management page or call our customer service at 1-877-482-5503 to deactivate this service.

2. For customers who signed up for our annual plan before 11/30/2007:

* You have received 200 free iTalkFreedom minutes every month while your annual plan is active. Outgoing calls through iTalkFreedom access numbers and incoming calls through your iTalkFreedom China Phone Number within those 200 minutes are still free.

* Minutes exceeding your free 200 minutes per month will be charged with our new per minute rates starting 12/24/2007. This includes both outgoing calls through iTalkFreedom access numbers and incoming calls through your iTalkFreedom China Phone Number.

3. For customers who signed up for our annual plan starting 12/1/2007 until now, will not be affected by this rate change when using iTalkFreedom service.

Thank you for your understanding!
