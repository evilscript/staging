iTalkBB and its Employees Support Gulf Coast Relief Efforts

(Vienna, VA, September 19)

09/19/2005

Establishes IP Based Calling Centers for Evacuees and Emergency Workers.

Hurricane Katrina has had a devastating effect on the US Gulf Coast region. In the wake of this disaster, the hearts, minds, energy, and talents of iTalkBB employees are intensely focused on assisting relief efforts.

iTalk Global Communications, Inc. (iTalkBB), the pioneer in the Voice over Internet Protocol (VoIP) industry, is taking steps to strengthen the emergency communications infrastructure and calling capabilities throughout the community. iTalkBB has established VoIP-based calling centers to give evacuees and emergency workers access to calling anywhere in the United States as needed.

The people of iTalkBB extend their sympathy to all those affected by Hurricane Katrina and wish to offer their support as the area recovers and begins to rebuild.
