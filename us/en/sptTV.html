iTalkBB TV installation steps:

  • Connect the TV (HDMI high-definition line is recommended for you to enjoy HD quality)

    >Use HDTV: Plug HDMI high-definition lines into the TV box and the HDMI port of the TV respectively；

    > Use traditional TV: If your TV does not have an HDMI port, insert the three plugs at each end of the AV composite video cable into the AV port with the same color as the plug, on the TV and TV box；

  • Connect to the network

    > Use the wireless network: Install the mast on the antenna base of the TV box；

    > Use a wired network: Insert both ends of the network cable into the TV box network interface and the modem;

  • Connect the power supply

    >Plug the power adapter into the TV box power connector and socket separately

  • Set up the network

    > Use a wired network: Connect the cable and turn on the box to watch TV.

    > Use the wireless network: Select "connect to the wireless network", and operate following the instructions on the TV box.

Videos on demand

unlimited popular movies, TV shows, variety shows, children's programs for you to access for free.

Live channels

iTalkBB Chinese TV provides you with dozens of Chinese and foreign live channels, enabling you to receive information and enjoy entertainments without any time difference.

72-hour playback

iTalkBB Chinese TV provides you with 72-hour playback service. It will record all the content within 3 days, so you will not miss any wonderful content. Please select the "TV service" section on the homepage and select the channel you want to see. Move the mouse to the name of the channel you want to watch, the program list within 72 hours of that channel will display.

Recent Browsing

Please enter the "My Box" section on the homepage and click "Recent Browsing" to find the latest 24 on-demand program you watched. The programs are in the reverse order of time. iTalkBB Chinese TV also provides you with the breakpoint feature to record what you have watched. When you watch the video next time, you can start at the point you stopped last time.

Play Your Own Video

Any local movie or picture can be played on the TV screen via iTalkBB Chinese TV. Connect your mobile device (such as a USB flash drive, mobile hard disk, etc.) to the USB jack of the TV box. Select "My TV Box" section on the homepage, then click "Play My Video" to access files on your mobile device, like browsing images, playing movies and listening to music within several clicks.

TV Functions Troubleshooting

There is no image display during the startup of TV box

  • Please check the connection of video cable and the network. Make sure only use one of the two lines among the three-color line and HDMI line, rather than connecting two lines
  • Please make sure the TV box has been turned on. The power light on the top of the TV box should be in green if the TV box works normally
  • Check the "Input" key or "Source" key to see if you switch to the correct video channel that has image input

After the TV box is installed, there is image display but no sound

Please check following the steps:

>If you are using a video/audio cable with colors of red, white and yellow, make sure the cable connect the correct left channel and right channel. Please connect the cable to the TV and the TV box according to the colors of the cable lines.

> Make sure the box is not muted and set the volume key to the maximum

>Check if the TV is in video mode and increase the TV volume to the maximum

>Check if the video/audio cable works normally. It is recommended to test the cable with other devices in your home(such as DVD player)

The TV display is in black and white

Please change the TV system to NTSC mode. When the TV is in PAL mode, it may be in black and white. Check the instruction manual of your TV for more details.

The video is frozen when watching TV

If there is only a frozen image on the screen but no sound

>Please exit the program and try another program; if it works well, and then exit and go back to the original one

>If you can not exit normally, try unplugging the power, wait about ten seconds, and restart the device

>If the above solutions does not work, check Network Connection Troubleshooting

The program is suddenly interrupted when watching TV

>This situation is usually due to the busy network, please exit the program, wait for a few minutes and then try again

>If it still does not work normally, there may be something wrong with the network. Go to "Network Settings" in "My Box" section to re-connect the network

The remote controller does not respond while video playing normally

>Replace the batteries of the remote controller and make sure the batteries are installed correctly

>Please check all buttons on the remote controller to see if there are broken buttons that do not bounce up.

Network Connection Troubleshooting

The homepage of iTalkBB Chinese TV displays, but you can not play any online video

Please check if the signal indicator in the upper right corner of your screen is in green. If it is a red cross, meaning you have not connected to the network. Go to "Network Settings" in "My Box" section to re-connect the network, then try it again. If it still does not work, please contact us.
