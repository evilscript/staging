Update Account Information

By logging into your online account, you can easily update your account information. Just follow the steps below:

>Login to your online account
Please note: First time users, login information is included in your order confirmation email. If you have not received the email, please contact us. If this is not your first-time, please use your username and password to log in.

>Click "Account & Bill" on the top and click "Update Account Info" in the "Account Profile" section to update your account information (address, email, contact number).

Forgetting Your Password

If you forget your password, please contact us. Our professional customer service staff will help you verify the identity. We will retrieve the password for you or help you reset it.

Account Suspended

Suspend your account for a certain period: You can suspend your account by contacting us.

Account suspended due to debt: If iTalkBB fails to charge your credit card on your billing day, your phone service will be suspended immediately. If you do not pay your bill for more than 45 days, your account will be revoked.

Payment Methods

We only accept debit and credit card payments, including Visa, Master, American Express and Discover.

Payment Date

The service cycle is every 30 days, the starting date of your bill period is when you activate your account. The following 30 days from the starting day are regarded as one bill period.We will automatically collect the monthly fee of your plan for the next month and extra service charges of the latest month.

Please note: If you are using both iTalkBB Mobile services and iTalkBB home phone services, we will regard your home phone bill period as your mobile service bill period as well. We will charge your mobile service fee from your activation date to your next period starting date.

Payment Failed

If iTalkBB fails to charge your debit or credit card, your service will be suspended. If you do not pay your bill for more than 45 days, your account and phone number will be revoked. Any balance in your account is non-refundable and can not be transferred to other accounts. To avoid this, please update your credit card information when needed. If you already have an unpaid bill in your account, please pay for it after updating your credit card information.

Check Your Bill

  • iTalkBB allows you to easily manage your bill by logging into your account online. After logging in, click "Billing Information" on the top to view the latest bill.
  • If you want to check previous bills, click "Billing information" in the top menu, and then go into the "History Billing" to access the bills for the last 6 months.

Read Your Bill

Bill period：After you activate your account, the following 30 days is regarded as one bill period.

Payment：You do not need to remember the date and pay the payment manually after receiving the bill. We will automatically collect the monthly fee of your plan for the next month and extra service charges of the latest month.

Using Both iTalkBB Mobile Services and iTalkBB Home Phone Services

After you activate iTalkBB Mobile services, we will regard your home phone bill period as your mobile service bill period as well. We will charge your mobile service fee from your activation date to your next period.
