The Yao Ming Foundation and iTalkBB Set Up New Free Hotline to Directly Reach Qinghai Earthquake Area!

(Vienna, VA, April 14)

04/14/2010

April 12, 2010 - A rapid series of strong earthquakes hit a mountainous and impoverished area of China's Qinghai province early Wednesday, killing 589 people and injuring over ten thousands. The Yao Ming Foundation and iTalkBB quickly responded to this disaster by providing a FREE hotline for the overseas Chinese to contact their friends and relatives in Qinghai.

Jack Zhao, the CEO of iTalkBB said "We are very sad about what happened in Qinghai and we wanted to provide this free hotline for overseas Chinese to help them to quickly reach their friends and relatives in Qinghai area and keep a close communication without any financial concern."

In response to the earthquake Yao Ming said, "I send my sincere condolences to the families of the victims. My foundation is determining how we can best help. We are pleased to be able to collaborate with iTalkBB on this free hotline to help relatives of the victims."
