All iTalkBB mobile plans offer unlimited data.

Unlimited data and high-speed data is different, each mobile plan provides limited high-speed 4G LTE data per month. When your 4G LTE high-speed data is used up, 3G data automatically kicks in, however, the speed will decline. Even if the speed slows down, it still enables you to send emails, browse online, and chat online normally.

For example, If a user is using the premium plan with 5GB high-speed data provided by iTalkBB mobile plan then they can use the high-speed data to download videos, pictures, send and receive emails, and browse online. Once they uses more than 5GB data, their network speed will decrease for the remaining billing period, but they can still use web services with iTalkBB mobile products.

Data transfer

Each month, all remaining high-speed data will rollover to the next billing period and the transferred data is valid for 3 months. When the 4G LTE high-speed data is used up, the transferred data is rolled over. If the transferred high-speed data is also used up, 3G data will also be available for them to access the Internet (US SIM card only).

Network Connection Failure

When you use mobile services within areas that are covered by 3G, 4G, or 4G LTE network, your mobile phone will automatically search for the best network. When the phone has successfully connected to the 3G,4G or 4G LTE network, 3G, 4G, H or 4G LTE will appear at the upper left corner or upper right corner your phone.

If your mobile phone fails to connect to any network, please check:

> Your mobile phone supports 3G, 4G, or 4G LTE network

> Your phone has the latest version of software

> There is mobile network in your area

> You restart your phone

> Your SIM card is inserted properly in the device

> You manually search network

> Other iTalkBB 3G, 4G, or 4G LTE mobile devices are working fine in your area

If you are still unable to connect to a mobile network, please visit our iTalkBB store, or call our 24/7 support at 1-855-482-5566, our professional customer service staff will assist you with any questions.

Unable to make or receive calls

Please check:

> If your mobile phone has no signal, please refer to Network Connection Failure

> You have paid all bills

> You turn off your cell phones for 5 seconds, and then restart it

> You remove SIM card, reload again

If the problem is still not resolved, please visit our iTalkBB store, or call our 24/7 support at 1-855-482-5566, our professional customer service staff will assist you with any questions.

Unable to dial a specific number

Please check:

> If you are using a third-party application or dial-up software (such as Google Voice, etc), please exit first

> If you are dialing an international number, please make sure you have enabled the international calling features and you are calling with correct country code

> You turn off your cell phones for 5 seconds, and then restart it

> If the problem occurs only when calling a specific number, please redial this number in call dialing interface rather than recent calls interface

> Try to use a different phone to dial a number to see if it properly connected

> If all of the above fails, please try to contact the operator of that number since there may be something wrong with that number; if the above test succeeds, please dial the number again using your own number: call *67 plus 11-digit number. If it works, then the reason you cannot connect to that number is that your number is blocked

If the problem is still not resolved, please visit our iTalkBB retail store, or call our 24/7 support at 1-855-482-5566, our professional customer service staff will assist you with any questions.

Poor call quality

Please make sure:

> Your phone has at least 2 bars of signal

> Don’t cover the microphone when you make calls

> Your phone has no special casing or protective film; if there is, remove it and try again

> You use your mobile phone without headphones (wired or wireless); if the problem occurs only when you wear headphones, then the poor call quality may be because of your headphones

> You do not turn on speaker

> You turn off your cell phones for 5 seconds, and then restart it

If the problem is still not resolved, please visit our iTalkBB retail store, or call our 24/7 support at 1-855-482-5566, our professional customer service staff will assist you with any questions.

What is 4G LTE?

ITalkBB national 4G LTE network allows you to enjoy the extraordinary network speed! Fast network speed makes your internet experience go smoothly and freely. 4G LTE network has 50% higher speed than 3G network. If you are within the coverage area of our 4G network, you can enjoy an unprecedented high-speed network. Now check the 4G coverage areas! * Frequency table: Band 2 (1900 MHz), Band 4 (1700/2100 MHz), Band 12 (700 MHz)

What is the 4G (HSPA+)?

The maximum download speed of 4G network is up to 21 Mbps to 42 Mbps. At such a high speed, your mobile experience will be wonderful. You can make phone calls, browse the web, download music and more. Frequencies: Band 2 (1900 MHz), Band 4 (1700/2100 MHz)

What is the 3G (HSPA)?

The maximum download speed of 3G network is up to 14 Mbps, with this speed you can make phone calls and use data services. Frequencies: Band 4 (1700/2100 MHz)

What is the 2G (GSM, GPRS, and EDGE)?

The download speed of ITalkBB 2G network is up to 263 Kbps. Traditional 2G network has a speed between 35 Kbps and 45kbps. With this speed, you can still easily navigate web pages, email, send and receive the app. Frequencies: Band 2 (1900 MHz)

Equipment requirements

The equipment you use has to support the network you use, for example, only cell phones that support 4G LTE network can achieve 4G LTE network speed. In addition, your location has to be within the network coverage area. Please click to view network coverage.
