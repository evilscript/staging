ICNTV CEO and Management Team make a U.S. visit with iTalkBB to partner to improve Chinese TV overseas

05/03/2016

CEO of ICNTV. Inc, Mr. Zhihui Xiong and his management team arrived in LA, USA on April 15th. They visited iTalkBB’s Arcadia store and had a meeting with Mr. Yuan Xiao, Group Vice President and Ms. Victoria Lei, Executive Vice President of iTalkBB, along with iTalkBB’s management team. During the meeting the iTalkBB and ICNTV representatives mapped out a partnership to boost the development of Chinese TV overseas.

ITalkBB and ICNTV Management Team

EVP of iTalkBB (Left) and CEO of ICNTV (Right)

iTalkBB was established 13 years ago and is a leader in the telecommunications industry, ranks among the top 5 VoIP companies in North America, and is the No. 1 telecom provider to global overseas Chinese. Its mission is to provide high quality products and service for overseas Chinese.

ICNTV, as the major components of China broadcast control platform, has always been insisting popularize legal authorized TV/movie contents in the world. This same hope is base of this cooperation.

ICNTV. Inc is a subsidiary of China Network Television (CNTV), the official online division of Chinese national public broadcaster China Central Television, and is also the first internet television platform approved by China's State Administration of Radio, Film and Television (SARFT).

Zhihui Xiong, CEO of ICNTV, had the following to say about the partnership, “iTalkBB is the telecommunication brand with great influence among overseas Chinese. iTalkBB deeply understands the habits and needs of the oversea Chinese since it based in oversea Chinese. We very much need to cooperate with enterprises like iTalkBB which both share the international vision and local realization, it will greatly improve the development of legally authorized TV contents in the world.”

Xiong spoke highly of this corporation

Victoria Lei , EVP of iTalkBB, said “iTalkBB is dedicated to provide better experience for the customers. We want to provide stable, high definition picture quality and abundant TV and movie resources to our customers through the platform the ICN TV provides. Through this partnership meeting, we are confident that we will be able to meet the customers’ expectation and provide them with an enjoyable oversea lives.”.

EVP of iTalkBB (Left) and CMO of ICNTV (Right)

iTalkBB Chinese TV, which not only provides many TV/Movies resources and high definition picture quality but also a complete operating system and customer service, has been the top provider of Chinese TV in North America since its establishment. Cooperating with ICN TV reflects iTalkBB’s respect of ICN TV’s intellectual property. The meeting represents a milestone of the partnership between both parties, which will trust and support each other, build up the legal content platform for the overseas Chinese, and provide overseas Chinese better experiences with higher quality TV entertainment resources. Additionally, it widens the road of Chinese culture spreading to the world.
