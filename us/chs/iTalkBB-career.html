iTalk Global Communications, Inc Mclean, Virginia

The Technical Support Specialist position is responsible for providing technical solutions to Tier 1 Customer Service Representatives and directly to customers by using excellent, in-depth knowledge of company products and programs as well as communicating effectively with team members within and outside the customer service department.

Your main focus will be on our exciting new mobile product. Knowledge of mobile phone service and handsets will be a large plus. When Tier 1 has tried standard support policies for customers, and there are still issues, they will escalate to you. You will be expected to use your best judgment and identify repeated problems as possible software bugs. Your job is to resolve problems too complicated for Tier 1 using tools we build to your specifications.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Respond to trouble tickets that have been escalated by Tier 1, responding quickly and taking ownership;
  • Work directly with customers trying to leave their old carrier to ensure rapid transfer over to us;
  • Resolve complex problems by researching and exploring answers and alternative solutions; implementing solutions and adding to knowledgebase;
  • Fulfills requests requiring 2nd level approval; use best judgment in giving customer refunds or bonuses;
  • Identify problem patterns and involve other departments to determine root cause;
  • Maintains call center database by entering information;
  • Updates job knowledge by participating in educational opportunities;
  • Enhances organization reputation by accepting ownership for accomplishing new and different requests; exploring opportunities to add value to job accomplishments;
  • Occasional coverage for off hour requests on a scheduled rotation;
  • Perform ad hoc tasks as needed.

Job Requirements:

  • High school diploma or GED required. A Bachelor's degree is a plus.
  • Fluent in Mandarin and comfortable in English.
  • Help desk experience is a plus.
  • EXCELLENT skills dealing with people.
  • Proficiency with computer applications.
  • Outstanding written and verbal communication skills.
  • Professional phone etiquette.
  • Must be eligible to work in the United States.
  • Background check will be required.
