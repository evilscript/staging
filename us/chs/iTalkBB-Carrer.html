Accountant (Chinese Mandarin Bilingual)

iTalk Global Communications, Inc Mclean, Virginia

This Accountant position will assist in the day-to-day activities of the Accounting/Finance department. The accountant will assist manager with monthly close activities. The position requires individual who is highly competent in organization and prioritizing tasks while staying committed to delivering quality work and meeting deadlines.

Major Responsibilities:

  • Prepare account receivables and account payables journal entries on a monthly basis.
  • Record customer check deposits and make bank deposits.
  • Process and prepare customer refund checks.
  • Maintain credit card chargeback accounts and process chargebacks.
  • Prepare daily cash reports for all banking activities.
  • Manage regular cash disbursements process with supervisors.
  • Perform ad hoc tasks.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Accounting required
  • 2-4 years of Accounting experience required in the office setting
  • Computer skills, with emphasis on Microsoft office
  • Ability to prioritize multiple projects/requests and operate independently with minimum daily supervision
  • Fluent in both English and Mandarin. Excellent verbal and written skills in both languages
  • Must be eligible to work in the United States.

Call Center Manager (Chinese Mandarin Bilingual)

iTalk Global Communications, Inc Mclean, Virginia

We are seeking a Mid-level manager or Call Center Manager with strong metrics management skills as well as call center management experience to establish and lead a small team of sales and customer care professional.

The Call Center Manager, is responsible for managing all aspects of iTalk's call center with a focus on performance and team development, best practice development and thought leadership as well as delivering against sales goal and budget. The Call Center Manger will be accountable for ensuring that quality service and operational excellence is provided to our customers.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Oversee the day-to-day management, coaching, and development of a team of Customer Service Representatives
  • Carrying out supervisory responsibilities in accordance with iTalk's policies and procedures;additional responsibilities include staffing, training and motivating employees; workforce planning, assigning and directing work; rewarding and disciplining employees; and effective conflict resolution.
  • Evaluates the quality of Customer Service Rep's calls from customers; provides feedback, coaching, and mentoring to reps on strengths and areas for improvement.
  • Collaborate with Marketing Department to continue to improve call center performance.
  • Responsible for customer experience and customer retention. Facilitate complex issue troubleshooting and resolution and serve as the point of escalation for customer support issues.
  • Tracks and reports contact center performance against objectives and goals (i.e. quality, call volume, customer satisfaction, etc.).
  • Special projects as needed.

Job Requirements:

  • At least 5 years of customer service experience
  • At least 3 years' experience managing others
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Fluent Mandarin and English speaker
  • Ability to research detailed, lengthy account information
  • Ability to navigate and use multiple systems
  • Quick thinker with the ability to multi-task
  • Detail oriented with the ability to easily track documentation
  • Self-motivated, driven and the ability to manage multiple priorities

呼叫中心主管:

岗位职责:

  • 负责呼叫中心的日常管理、指导和团队的建设。
  • 根据公司的政策和章程履行监督职责：其中责任包括：人员配备、安排业务培训、销售指导和员工激励；安排人员规划、分配和指导工作；奖励和惩戒员工；以及有效的解决冲突矛盾。
  • 建立客服部门的工作标准和程序，安排部门的战略部署和预算。
  • 评估客服代表的电话质量，提供反馈和指导。
  • 与市场部合作，持续提升呼叫中心的业绩
  • 负责客户管理和客户关系的维护。作为主管来帮助解决复杂的问题。
  • 根据目标和销售指标，汇报呼叫中心的业绩（比如：通话质量、呼叫量、和客户满意度等）
  • 负责上级领导安排的其他工作

能力要求：

  • 具备优秀的语言表达和书面沟通能力
  • 熟练掌握中文和英文
  • 具备良好的数据分析能力，能够理解复杂的账户信息
  • 具有能够记录和浏览多个系统的能力
  • 注重细节，能够轻松跟踪文档
